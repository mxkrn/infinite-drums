The infinite syncopation of the drum sequencer implemented on this page is based on the Regroove expressive drum sequencing engine. Regroove was originally built to be used in a Max for Live device that generates expressive drum rhythms using a learning understanding of groove. The representation of groove, learned by the embedded neural network, includes dynamics, microtiming, and syncopation and is ultimately based on nearly 1200 hours of drum rhythms played by session drummers on a 9-piece electronic drum kit. As this is just a demo, there's no interactivity and we have only encapsulated the syncopation model of Regroove - to get some groove in your rhythms you're gonna have to download the full device!

Visit the project page to find out more about Regroove!
